The average inflation rate in the Sudan was forecast to continuously decrease between 2024 and 2029 by in total 137.2 percentage points. According to this forecast in 2029, the inflation will have decreased for the sixth consecutive year to 8.34 percent. This indicator measures inflation based upon the year on year change in the average consumer price index. The latter expresses a country's average level of prices based on a typical basket of consumer goods and services.
